## Full description (max 4000 characters)

**PDF Craft is your complete PDF & document toolbox — fast, private, and packed with every tool you need to work with PDFs on the go.**

Whether you're merging reports, signing a contract, scanning a receipt, converting a PDF to Word, or just reading a document, PDF Craft does it all in one clean, modern app. No clutter, no sign-up to get started — just powerful tools that work.

**📄 Organize & edit PDFs**
• Merge multiple PDFs into one
• Split & extract pages by range
• Reorder, rotate, crop and duplicate pages
• Add or remove blank pages
• Add page numbers, headers & footers
• Edit PDF metadata and bookmarks

**🔄 Convert anything**
• PDF → Word, Excel, PowerPoint
• PDF → JPG images
• Images → PDF
• Image Studio: compress, resize, convert & apply filters to photos

**🗜️ Optimize & enhance**
• Compress PDFs to shrink file size
• Optimize & flatten documents
• Grayscale conversion
• Remove blank pages
• N-up layout (multiple pages per sheet)

**✍️ Annotate & sign**
• Draw, highlight and add text & sticky notes
• Sign PDFs with your signature
• Stamp, watermark & QR-code stamp
• Fill PDF forms
• Redact sensitive content

**🔒 Secure your files**
• Password-protect & unprotect PDFs
• Repair damaged PDFs

**📷 Scan on the go**
• Scan physical documents with your camera and save them as crisp PDFs

**🔍 Read & extract**
• Smooth built-in PDF viewer with night mode and jump-to-page
• Extract text from PDFs
• Open any PDF in an external viewer with one tap

**🗂️ Powerful file manager**
• Browse your storage with fast filtering & sorting (name, type, size, date)
• Search every file with type filters
• Recent files & favorites at your fingertips
• Select files across folders and apply a tool in one go
• Open & share PDFs straight from other apps ("Open with PDF Craft")

**☁️ Google Drive**
• Connect your Drive, browse your files, and use them with any tool — or upload and share

**⚡ Batch processing**
• Apply a tool to many PDFs at once and save time

**🎨 Built for you**
• Clean, modern interface with Light, Dark & System themes
• Works offline for local tools — your files stay on your device

Download PDF Craft and turn your phone into a complete PDF workstation. 🚀

## Release notes — extended (changelog)

### Version 2.0.0

**New**
- File manager overhaul: filter and sort by name, extension, size and date with ascending/descending order.
- Cross-folder multi-select — pick files from anywhere and apply a single- or multi-file tool via smart suggestions.
- Reworked search: shows recents when empty, type filters, sort, in-progress indicator, and per-file actions.
- "Open with PDF Craft" and share-to-app support for PDFs and images, with a clean chooser screen.
- Google Drive: browse cloud files and use them directly with any tool, plus upload & share.
- Recents & Favorites redesigned with real PDF thumbnails and quick actions.
- Tool search and a "Recently used" tools row.

**Improved**
- Redesigned, lightweight splash screen and skippable intro (replay anytime from Settings).
- Cleaner locked/encrypted-PDF screen with quick "Enter password" and "Open externally".
- Live theme switching (Light / Dark / System) without restarting.
- Consistent, modern filter & sort controls across Files and Search.

**Fixed**
- Crash when cancelling dialogs (rename, password, go-to-page).
- Uppercase ".PDF" files now open in the in-app viewer.
- "Page not found" when opening a file from another app.
- Layout crash in the selection bar and dialog buttons.
- Various stability and performance improvements.
